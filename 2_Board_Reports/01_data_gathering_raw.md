### Key Input Challenges
1. High import duties on tanning chemicals
2. Energy cost inconsistency (gas/power)
3. Quality variability of raw hides/skins
4. Logistical bottlenecks (road/port infrastructure)
5. FX volatility impacting import costs
6. China = 88% of footwear imports into Pakistan (competitive pressure)

### Industry SWOT Summary
| Strengths | Weaknesses |
|---|---|
| $5.8B domestic market | Intense China/India/Vietnam competition |
| Low labor costs | Technological gaps (SME sector) |
| Quality leather & craftsmanship | Inconsistent quality standards |
| Manufacturing clusters | Export decline in FY24 |
