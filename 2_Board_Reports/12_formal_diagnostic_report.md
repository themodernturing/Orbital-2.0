## 2. THE METHODOLOGY: WHY PROXIES WORK

Top-tier management consulting often utilizes "Sector-Adjusted Proxies" when dealing with private entities. Our rationale for using Bata (PKR 18B) and Service (PKR 125B) as benchmarks for Imperial is based on three pillars:

### I. Ratios Transcend Scale
Whether a firm operates 20 stores or 400, the physics of footwear retail in Pakistan (seasonal clearance, import lead times, and working capital cycles) remain constant. If the industry leader (Bata) maintains an Inventory Turnover of 3.5x, a mid-tier player lacking the same economy-of-scale leverage is almost certainly operating below this "Efficiency Frontier."

### II. The Competitor Ceiling
Imperial Footwear competes for the same Tier-1 mall space (Packages Mall, LuckyOne) as Bata and Stylo. Landlords measure value in **Revenue Per Square Foot**. By using the peer throughput as a "Ceiling," we identify the exact delta in brand extraction efficiency.

### III. Diagnostic Bait
In high-stakes consulting, these quantified estimates serve as "Diagnostic Bait." By presenting a probability-weighted lockup figure (e.g., PKR 300M), we move the C-Suite from a defensive posture to a diagnostic one, forcing an internal audit of the real vs. modeled gap.
