# 3. System Architecture

Orbital Internal operates on a deterministic modeling engine. All scoring logic is locked. AI translates output into executive-grade insight but does not modify formulas.

---

## A. External Physics Layer
Orbital synthesizes trade flow signals, sector demand proxies, competitive density modeling, and revenue-per-asset benchmarking. This layer exists outside ERP systems.

---

## B. Capital Velocity Engine
Orbital quantifies working capital intensity variance, revenue density imbalance, and asset productivity deviation to produce a **Capital Velocity Map**.

---

## C. Strategic Ranking Engine
Orbital ranks capital reallocation moves using ROI-adjusted modeling, probability-weighted impact, and risk exposure scoring. Each move includes quantified impact projections.

---

## D. Forward Simulation Engine
Before execution, Orbital models forward outcomes:
* Cash release projection
* Revenue uplift delta
* Margin trajectory shift
* Capital redeployment IRR

---

## E. Continuous Recalibration Layer
Once deployed, Orbital provides monthly ranking updates, signal drift detection, and board-ready intelligence memos. It becomes ongoing capital governance infrastructure.
