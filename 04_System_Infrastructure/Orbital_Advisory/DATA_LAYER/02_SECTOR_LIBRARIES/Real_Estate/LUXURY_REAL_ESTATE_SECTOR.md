## Sector Profile

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Primary Driver | Asset development + brand-led premium positioning |
| Revenue Model | Mixed: Capital gains (residential sales) + Recurring (hotel fees, mall rent, property management) |
| Key Risk | Oil/commodity dependency in resource economies, brand partner exit, construction bottlenecks |
| Competitive Moat | International brand partnerships (management agreements), location premium, first-mover scale |
| Data Availability | Low (private developers), Medium (publicly listed REITs), High (hotel brands via Booking.com/TripAdvisor) |

| Digital/Innovation | Website quality, virtual tour capability, PropTech adoption, online sales channels | Direct audit vs. Emaar/global peers |
