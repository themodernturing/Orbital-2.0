## Resolution Principles

1. **Never argue.** Show the data trail.
2. **If we were wrong:** Acknowledge it, update the Data Register, and adjust the report.
3. **If we were right but lacked evidence:** Note the gap and request internal data access.
4. **If it's ambiguous:** Downgrade the confidence level and add a disclaimer.

Every dispute makes the next report better.
