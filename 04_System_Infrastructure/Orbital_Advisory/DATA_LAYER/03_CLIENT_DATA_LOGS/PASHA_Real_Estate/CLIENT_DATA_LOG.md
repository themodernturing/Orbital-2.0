## Assumptions Made

1. Employee count (22,000+) includes all PASHA Holding subsidiaries — not isolated to real estate
2. Mall occupancy (99%) is self-reported and not independently verified
3. Ownership structure documented by international media — not independently verified
4. Brand visibility measured by public review platforms — may not reflect full marketing reach
5. Revenue mix estimates are structural modeling, not actual financial data (private company)
6. Brand premium values based on comparable markets, not PASHA-specific internal pricing
7. Oil shock cascade based on 2014-2016 historical data — future shocks may differ
8. Construction dependency intensity (90%) is estimated from public evidence of no contractor diversification
9. Scenario probabilities reflect structural modeling under defined assumptions, not forecasts
