## Resolution Rules

1. **Check the NDI Claim Ledger first** — was this claim already flagged as DIVERGENT or UNVERIFIED?
2. **If we were wrong:** Acknowledge, update Data Register, add lesson to Playbook Section 7
3. **If we were right but evidence was weak:** Upgrade the evidence or downgrade the confidence
4. **If self-correction is needed:** Mark it explicitly in the next version (Lesson L15 — visible self-correction builds trust)

Every dispute that generates a Playbook lesson makes the entire system smarter.
