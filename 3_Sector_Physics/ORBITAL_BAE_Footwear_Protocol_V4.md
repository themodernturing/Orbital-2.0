## 3. The Protocol: Mapping Signals to PE Priorities

We do not just report data; we translate "Outside-In" signals into the financial metrics that drive your Investment Committee (IC) decisions.

### 3.1 Throughput Verification $\rightarrow$ **Revenue Durability**
*   **Signal**: Aggregated Customs Manifests vs. Claimed Sales Growth.
*   **Diligence Value**: Detects "Channel Stuffing" or inventory stagnation. If import volumes are dropping while sales claims are rising, revenue durability is compromised.

### 3.2 Yield Gap Analysis $\rightarrow$ **Margin Stability & Capital Intensity**
*   **Signal**: Store-level throughput vs. Public Peer Physics (RPSF - Revenue per Sq Ft).
*   **Diligence Value**: Identifies "Trapped Capital." If the target turns stock at 1.5x while the peer standard is 3.5x, we quantify the immediate EBITDA upside achievable through inventory professionalization.

### 3.3 Sentiment Integrity $\rightarrow$ **Brand Terminal Value (Exit Optionality)**
*   **Signal**: Audited External Sentiment Indices vs. Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) Proxies.
*   **Diligence Value**: Assesses brand decay. A high sentiment gap indicates a "dying" brand that will suffer from margin compression and low exit terminal value.
