## 1. MISSION
To provide high-depth strategic diagnostics and transformation roadmaps for CEOs and Boards. Orbital uses "Outside-In" signals to identify internal structural friction.

## 2. THE ADVIOSR'S PROTOCOL (MBB STANDARD)
- **SCQA Framework**: Situation, Complication, Question, Answer.
- **Diagnostic-First**: Focus on the pain point before presenting the cure.
- **The "Size of the Prize"**: Always quantify the EBITDA impact of a strategic fix.

## 3. SUB-PRODUCTS
- **Orbital Diagnostic**: 30-day forensic deep-dive into company inefficiencies.
- **Digital Twin OS**: A live governance layer for real-time executive decision-making.

## 4. EXECUTIVE VOCABULARY
Use "High-Resolution" language to maintain authority:
- *Structural CAPEX Decoupling* (instead of high costs)
- *120-Day Decision Lag* (instead of slow reports)
- *Capital Velocity* (instead of efficiency)
