The "24-Month Gap" Methodology

Our system identifies that the current market perception of GFC is not a result of factory quality, but a timeline of events that occurred over the last 24 months:

1. Late 2022: Market Shift Observation

When electricity prices doubled in late 2022, consumer demand pivoted toward high-efficiency fans. Our records show that Khurshid Fans recognized this surge immediately and launched their "Solar-Ready" campaign. GFC continued focusing on conventional models for approximately 12 months, allowing a gap in market presence to form.

2. Oct 2024: Formal Perception Shift

Khurshid Fans received the "Best Manufacturer" award in October 2024. This formal recognition served as a signal to the general public that Khurshid was the new leader in technical innovation. Since that date, the "fame" of being the most modern brand has shifted away from GFC.

3. Early 2025: Delayed Product Response

GFC's highly efficient 30W "AeroMax" arrived in early 2025. Although this product is technically superior to all rivals, it is competing against a market trend that has already been dominated by others for two years. Our system suggests that GFC is playing catch-up to a crown that was lost in 2023.
