| **Step 1: In-Principle Approval** | Bank submits application to Director, BPRD (copy to Director, IBD). | **Step 2: Branch Conversion** | Systematic conversion of all conventional branches to Islamic branches, following branch-level conversion criteria (Annual Branch Conversion Plan, customer notification 3.5 months advance, customer consent/dissent mechanisms, virtual conventional cost centers for unconvertible accounts) | FABL converted branches in annual phases: 59 (2019) + 65 (2020) + continued through 2021–22.
