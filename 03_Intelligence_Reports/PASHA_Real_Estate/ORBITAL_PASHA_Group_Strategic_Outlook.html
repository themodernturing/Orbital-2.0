Section 04 — Capital Velocity Model
Capital Break-Even: When Does the Investment Pay Back?
PASHA has deployed an estimated ~$1.2–1.4B into Crescent Bay and related assets. Capital velocity measures how quickly that investment converts into cumulative revenue, and when the crossover point arrives.
ORBITAL PREDICTION
Cumulative revenue is projected to cross cumulative capital deployed between 2034–2037 under base case. Under accelerated conditions, this compresses to 2032–2033. This is typical for mega mixed-use projects — Dubai Marina took 15+ years to break even.
